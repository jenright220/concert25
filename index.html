About John Williams

John Williams is one of the most successful and recognized film composers in Hollywood's history. He has composed the music for numerous iconic films, including Star Wars, Indiana Jones, E.T., and of course, the Harry Potter series. His skillful use of leitmotifs and memorable melodies has made his work beloved worldwide.
